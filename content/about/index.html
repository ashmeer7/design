        Based out of New York and Boston, I am a 20-year-old student at Harvard University. I use photography, graphic design, digital illustration and video to make the beauty of the world tangible. Embarking on Saad Amer Design, I want to expose the elegance and simplicity of the forms we come across daily.

        My works have been published by Vogue Italia, Bund Magazine (China), Samsung USA, Star Central Magazine, Tuesday Magazine and more. In addition, I have received the Congressional Artist Award from the U.S. House of Representatives, won an $80,000 First Place Prize from Samsung Solve For Tomorrow Environmental Video Contest and was a Finalist in the United States Environmental Protection Agency Rachel Carson Photography Contest. I have also worked as the Creative Director of Jalato and as the Image Director for TheUnsungHeroine.

        Regarding Saad Amer Design, people often ask what exactly it is that I design. With an elated sense of what the world looks like, my work seeks to refine reality for its viewers. My images evaporate the nebulosity out of our immediacy, and expose a distilled sense of forthrightness about the world we live in. I ultimately design the space I am consumed in by regurgitating beauty from the stimuli it provides me with.
